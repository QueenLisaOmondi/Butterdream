About Our Bakery

Welcome to Butterdream, Kilifi's premier artisanal bakery since 2015. Our passion for quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques shines through in every bite.

All our products are made fresh daily using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. We take pride in our craft and hope you can taste the love in every creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours in Kilifi?

We deliver daily from 9am to 6pm within Kilifi and surrounding areas. Orders placed after 3pm will be delivered the next day.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, all major credit cards, and cash on delivery.

Can I place a custom order?

Yes! We specialize in custom cakes and pastries. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for special requests.

Do you offer catering for events?

Absolutely! We provide pastry platters and dessert tables for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions throughout Kilifi County.
